LDFA:lateral distal femoral angle

MPTA: medial proximal tibial angle

TCO: Tibial Coronal Alignment

FCO: Femoral Coronal Alignment

HKA: hip-knee angle. Negative indicates varus, positive indicates valgus
HKA = MPTA - LDFA
HKA = 90 - TCO

JLO: joint line obliquity, > 180 indicates apex proximal, < 180 indicates apex distal
JLO = MPTA + LDFA
JLO = 90 + FCO
